Assassin Creed Origins




About The Game


Ancient Egypt. Once a prosperous and influential empire, now a crumbling civilization. Delve into this mysterious part of history and uncover the thrilling and dangerous tale behind the creation of the Assassins Brotherhood.

Journey through a country with the most diverse landscapes and wildlife ever seen in an Assassins Creed game. From the lush banks of the Nile to the hot deserts, you will come across forgotten tombs and legendary pyramids and encounter renowned figures.

Explore the story of the birth of the Brotherhood in a captivating adventure that takes place more than 2000 years before the present day.

Engage in numerous different quests to level up and unlock new skills. Each quest is a new story to explore. Craft your weapons to play the way you want it and master a whole new fight system.

Title: Assasin Creed Origins

Genre: Action
Developer: Ubisoft
Publisher: Ubisoft
Release Date: 23 Oct, 2017
